Data Science Manager Role

Your Roadmap to Becoming a Data Science Manager

Introduction

Data Science Managers oversee teams of data scientists and analysts, driving strategic decision-making and managing large-scale data projects.

Required Skills

  • Leadership skills and team management experience
  • Strong technical expertise in machine learning and data analysis
  • Excellent communication and collaboration abilities
  • Experience with project management and Agile methodologies
  • Ability to work with stakeholders to define business requirements

Experience

Entry-Level: 0-2 years

Mid-Level: 2-5 years

Senior-Level: 5+ years
