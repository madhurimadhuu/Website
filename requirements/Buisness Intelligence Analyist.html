Business Intelligence Analyst Role

Your Roadmap to Becoming a Business Intelligence Analyst

Introduction

Business Intelligence Analysts use data analysis tools to generate insights and reports that support business decisions.

Required Skills

  • Proficiency in BI tools like Tableau, Power BI, and Qlik
  • Strong knowledge of SQL and database management
  • Experience with data visualization techniques
  • Ability to work with large datasets and perform data mining
  • Strong communication and presentation skills

Experience

Entry-Level: 0-2 years

Mid-Level: 2-5 years

Senior-Level: 5+ years
