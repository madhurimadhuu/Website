AI Researcher Role

Your Roadmap to Becoming an AI Researcher

Introduction

AI Researchers focus on developing new algorithms and methodologies for artificial intelligence, often working at the frontier of the field.

Required Skills

  • Strong background in computer science and mathematics
  • Proficiency in AI algorithms and deep learning
  • Experience with AI research papers and publications
  • Proficiency in Python, TensorFlow, PyTorch
  • Understanding of data structures and algorithms

Experience

Entry-Level: 0-2 years

Mid-Level: 2-5 years

Senior-Level: 5+ years
