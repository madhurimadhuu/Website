Big Data Engineer Role

Your Roadmap to Becoming a Big Data Engineer

Introduction

Big Data Engineers are responsible for designing, building, and managing the infrastructure that supports the processing and analysis of large-scale data. They work with distributed computing systems like Hadoop, Spark, and NoSQL databases to handle massive datasets.

Required Skills

  • Proficiency in Hadoop, Spark, and other distributed systems
  • Strong programming skills in Java, Python, or Scala
  • Experience with databases such as NoSQL (Cassandra, MongoDB) and SQL (PostgreSQL, MySQL)
  • Knowledge of cloud platforms like AWS, Azure, or Google Cloud
  • Understanding of data pipelines, ETL processes, and data warehousing
  • Familiarity with data security practices and compliance

Experience

Entry-Level: 0-2 years

Mid-Level: 2-5 years

Senior-Level: 5+ years
