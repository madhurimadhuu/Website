AI Specialist Role

Your Roadmap to Becoming an AI Specialist

Introduction

AI Specialists work on developing cutting-edge artificial intelligence systems that can learn from data and adapt over time, solving complex real-world problems.

Required Skills

  • Strong knowledge of AI algorithms, machine learning, and deep learning
  • Proficiency in Python, Java, and TensorFlow
  • Experience with AI platforms such as AWS or Azure
  • Knowledge of cloud computing and distributed systems

Experience

Entry-Level: 0-2 years

Mid-Level: 2-5 years

Senior-Level: 5+ years
