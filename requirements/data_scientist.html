Data Scientist Role

Your Roadmap to Becoming a Data Scientist

Introduction

Data Scientists analyze complex datasets to extract actionable insights. They combine programming, statistics, and domain expertise to make informed decisions.

Required Skills

  • Strong knowledge in Python, R, or SQL
  • Experience with machine learning algorithms
  • Deep understanding of statistics and data analysis
  • Data visualization tools (e.g., Tableau, Power BI)
  • Experience in Big Data platforms (e.g., Hadoop, Spark)

Experience

Entry-Level: 0-2 years

Mid-Level: 2-5 years

Senior-Level: 5+ years
